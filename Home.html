Wardens Of The Red Tower
Did you ever wish that you could have been a knight from the medieval ages Riding on horseback, clad in steel armor? Participating in all forms of combat and show of skills? Well thankfully, you are not alone.
Over the past few years, a new sport has developed that is bringing back the skills that once kept fighters alive on the battle field. That sport is Historical Medieval Combat, or HMB.
The Wardens of the Red Tower were founded in 2016. We focus on weapon skills, physical fitness, teamwork and most importantly, Having Fun.
Over time you'll learn to fight with swords, shields, pole-arms and as part of a melee team. So come on by, and come learn the skills of a knight.
Practices are held weekly on Sundays @ 2:30 pm to 4:00 pm. All are welcome! (Yes, kids too!)
Practice location is Blakiston, Park (weather depending)
During winter months, the practice location is a private residence. If you wish to attend a practice, contact us using the links below.
Due to Covid-19, we require all participants to bring their own equipment, and all participants must wear a facemask for the duration of practice.
For a list of equipment, please contact us.
